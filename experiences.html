Unleash the Thrill: F-Xperience - Experiences

Welcome to the heart of F-Xperience, where your passion for speed, luxury, and precision comes to life. Our curated driving experiences are designed to immerse you in the world of supercars and exotic cars, offering unparalleled access to the machines that make your heart race. Whether you're looking to fulfill a lifelong dream, celebrate a special occasion, or simply indulge in your love for high-performance vehicles, we have the perfect experience waiting for you.

Our Signature Experiences

The Supercar Sprint

  • Duration: Half-day experience
  • Cars Featured: Choose from Ferrari, Lamborghini, McLaren, and more
  • Track: Iconic circuits and exclusive race tracks
  • Description: Feel the adrenaline rush as you take control of a supercar, learning to harness its power with the guidance of our professional instructors. This experience focuses on high-speed laps, precision driving techniques, and the pure exhilaration of pushing the limits.

Exotic Car Endurance

  • Duration: Full-day experience
  • Cars Featured: Includes rare models from Aston Martin, Porsche, and more
  • Track: Prestigious international circuits with challenging layouts
  • Description: Dive deep into the world of exotic cars with an experience that tests your endurance. Perfect your driving skills over longer sessions, enjoy comprehensive one-on-one coaching, and explore the capabilities of these extraordinary vehicles

The Ultimate Track Day

  • Duration: Customizable
  • Cars Featured: Access to our entire fleet, including exclusive, limited-edition models
  • Track: Your choice of world-renowned tracks
  • Description: Tailor your dream track day with complete freedom to select the cars and track of your choice. This premium experience includes advanced driving instruction, telemetry analysis, and the opportunity to drive some of the rarest cars in the world.

Night Drives & Light Shows

  • Duration: Evening experience
  • Cars Featured: LED-lit supercars and neon exotics
  • Track: Special night-time circuits with illuminated paths
  • Description: Experience the thrill of driving under the stars with our Night Drives & Light Shows. The track comes alive with lights, adding an extra layer of magic to your drive. Perfect for those looking to combine high-speed thrills with stunning visuals.
